 When a scoring request is initiated, it is first directed to ExclusiveGateway0, which then sends a delay task to ExclusiveGateway1. Simultaneously, ExclusiveGateway1 processes the scoring request and forwards the result against take to ExclusiveGateway2. Meanwhile, StartEvent1 triggers ExclusiveGateway2 to send a reply and also initiates Level1 scoring, which is routed to ExclusiveGateway2. Upon receiving the reply, ExclusiveGateway2 reports any delay and proceeds to Level2 scoring, which is then sent to ExclusiveGateway2. Finally, the Level2 scoring results are sent back as a Result\nsent.
