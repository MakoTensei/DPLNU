 A guest enters the Pieper court, triggering the process to prepare and deliver food. Once the food is ready, the guest is satisfied and their hunger is distilled. The process starts with the guest entering, followed by the selection of a court, adding the order, and selecting the Court of Justice. At the Court of Justice, the guest's order is taken and food is prepared. The food is then ready and the guest is satisfied, leading to the distillation of their hunger. The process ends with the guest's satisfaction and the food being made available.
