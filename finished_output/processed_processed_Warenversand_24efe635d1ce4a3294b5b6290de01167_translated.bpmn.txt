 The Secretariat first checks whether the incoming goods are in large or small quantities through ParallelGateway5. Once this is done, the goods are then processed for shipping, which involves the creation of a standardized parcel certificate through ExclusiveGateway2. After the goods are shipped, they go through a pickup process for Spedition 1, Spedition 2, and Spedition 3, all of which utilize ParallelGateway3. The goods are then packaged through ParallelGateway5 and are ready for collection. Before collection, the Secretariat checks if insurance is required through ExclusiveGateway2. If insurance is needed, it is completed through ExclusiveGateway2. Finally, the goods are ready for collection, which involves Goods ready for collection through ExclusiveGateway1 and ParallelGateway4.
