 1. Start with addressing hunger by selecting a court to serve the meal.
2. Add your order, specifying that you're not hungry at the moment.
3. Wait in line until it's your turn.
4. Receive a Pieper, who will prepare your meal.
5. The Pieper becomes active and starts preparing the meal.
6. The meal is ready, and the Pieper hands it over to you.
7. The Pieper explains the meal to you.
8. The meal is cut for preparation.
9. Trigger the Pieper to serve the meal.
10. The meal is served to you, and the order is completed.

This sequence of events ensures a smooth process from addressing hunger to the completion of the meal order.
