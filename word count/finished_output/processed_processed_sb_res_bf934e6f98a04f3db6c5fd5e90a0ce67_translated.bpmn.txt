 A customer feels hunger and decides to order food.

1. The customer places an order at the employee, triggering the process to select a court.
2. The order is accepted by the system, and the kitchen receives the message to prepare the meal.
3. The kitchen begins preparing the meal, and the process is judged as completed in the Empire.
4. The prepared food is handed over to the guest, who then picks up their meal.
5. The guest receives a message about Pieper, which is then sent to the customers.
6. If the customer does not react after 5 minutes, the system sends a message to Koch.
7. The process ends when the food is prepared and handed over to the guest.
