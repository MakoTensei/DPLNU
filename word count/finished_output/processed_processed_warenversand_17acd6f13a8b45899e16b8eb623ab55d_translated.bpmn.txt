 In the insurance process, the workflow begins with the 'StartEvent0' which triggers the 'Insurance close' task. This task is connected to the 'ExclusiveGateway1', which then leads to the 'ParallelGateway7'. The 'ParallelGateway7' is responsible for the 'Packaging goods' task, which in turn connects to the 'ParallelGateway6'. The 'ParallelGateway6' is linked to the 'Filling the package note' task, which is connected to the 'ExclusiveGateway2'. The 'ExclusiveGateway2' leads to the 'Choose your favorite' task, which connects to the 'Specialist'. The 'Specialist' then connects to the 'ExclusiveGateway3', which finally leads to the 'EndEvent0'.

In the same insurance process, the 'Offer 1' task is triggered by the 'StartEvent0' and connects to the 'ParallelGateway4'. The 'ParallelGateway4' leads to the 'Offer 2' task, which is connected to the 'ParallelGateway5'. The 'ParallelGateway5' leads to the 'Choose your favorite' task, which connects to the 'Specialist'. The 'Specialist' then connects to the 'ExclusiveGateway3', which finally leads to the 'EndEvent0'.

The 'Check quantity' task is triggered by the 'StartEvent0' and connects to the 'ExclusiveGateway1'. The 'ExclusiveGateway1' leads to the 'ParallelGateway7', which is connected to the 'Provide goods for collection' task. The 'Provide goods for collection' task connects to the 'EndEvent0'.

Lastly, the 'Packaging goods' task is triggered by the 'ParallelGateway6' and connects to the 'ParallelGateway7'. The 'ParallelGateway7' is connected to the 'EndEvent0'.
