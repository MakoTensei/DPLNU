1. The shipment of goods initiates the process and is directed to ExclusiveGateway4.
2. ExclusiveGateway4 receives the shipment of goods and checks if insurance is necessary.
3. If insurance is deemed necessary, the process continues to ExclusiveGateway5.
4. ExclusiveGateway5 then sends the shipment to ParallelGateway9.
