 When a special shipment is received, the process begins with the 'StartEvent0' which triggers the 'Select your offer' task. This task then connects to the 'ExclusiveGateway3' to appoint forwarding. Concurrently, the 'Filling the package note' task is initiated, also connecting to 'ExclusiveGateway2'. Once the insurance is closed, the process moves to 'ParallelGateway5' for the 'EndEvent0'. Meanwhile, the 'Appoint forwarding' task connects to 'ExclusiveGateway3' and the 'Packaging goods' task connects to 'ParallelGateway5'. Finally, the 'Provide goods for collection' task concludes the process at 'EndEvent0'.
