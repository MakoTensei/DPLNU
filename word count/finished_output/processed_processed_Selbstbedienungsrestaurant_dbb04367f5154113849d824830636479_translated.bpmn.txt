 1. Visit the restaurant to satisfy your hunger.
2. Select a table by choosing "Select a table."
3. Place your order by adding it to the menu.
4. Pay for your meal by providing payment.
5. Receive your food by picking it up from the kitchen.
6. Enjoy your meal at the table.
7. After finishing your meal, inform the staff that you're done.
8. The staff will then prepare your bill and process the payment.
9. Once your bill is settled, the staff will clean and clear your table.
