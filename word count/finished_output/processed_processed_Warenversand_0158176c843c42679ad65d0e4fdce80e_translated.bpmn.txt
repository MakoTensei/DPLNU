 Upon receiving goods, the process begins with the selection and assignment of the items, which is directed towards the InclusiveGateway2. Concurrently, the goods are also routed to ParallelGateway3 for parallel processing. Once the insurance is completed, the goods are sent to ParallelGateway3 for pickup. Meanwhile, the filling of the stand and package tickets is processed through InclusiveGateway2. After the goods are packaged, they are again sent to ParallelGateway3 for pickup. Finally, the pickup event is triggered, which involves the collection of the filled stand and package tickets.
