This process involves the 'Edit special shipment' task, which is triggered by the 'ParallelGateway3' and results in the 'ParallelGateway4'. The 'Fill in packaging paper' task, triggered by 'ExclusiveGateway0', leads to 'ExclusiveGateway2'. The 'Packaging goods' task, initiated by 'ParallelGateway3', also results in 'EndEvent0'. The 'Insurance close' task, triggered by 'ExclusiveGateway2', is followed by 'ParallelGateway4'.
